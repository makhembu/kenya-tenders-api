# Phase 13 — Business Model Projections (BakiPay)

## 1. Pricing Strategy
- **Base Fee:** KES 500 per month for managed group ledger tracking.
- **Success Fee:** KES 50 per successful B2C payout request (min 10 payouts/cycle).
- **Target ARPU:** KES 1,500/month per Chama.

## 2. Growth Roadmap (Month 1–12)
- **Q1 (Validation):** 10 Test Chamas. Focus on manual onboarding, stability, and math accuracy.
- **Q2 (Niche Expansion):** 50 Groups. Partner with local Sacco auditors and administrative secretaries.
- **Q3 (Growth):** 200 Groups. Introduce automated WhatsApp reminders for members.
- **Q4 (Scaling):** 500+ Groups. Expand to Western Kenya and Coastal informal economies.

## 4. Key Metrics (The North Star)
- **Calculation Accuracy:** 100% (Any deviation kills the business).
- **Payout Success Rate:** > 99%.
- **Retention:** > 90% after the first dividend cycle (The "Aha" moment).
